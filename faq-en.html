Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to rent a wedding dress?

The cost of renting a wedding dress ranges from 2,500–6,000 NIS. Our dresses are divided into 3 categories: a newly tailored dress with personal design, a new ready-made collection dress, and a collection of dresses worn up to 3 times. The price includes the dress, veil, petticoat, all alterations, ironing and cleaning.

What if I want to keep the dress?

If you wish to purchase the dress, the cost includes an additional 50% of the rental price.

How many appointments are needed before the wedding?

For a ready-made / collection dress - 3 appointments. For a custom-tailored design - 4 appointments.

When can I take the dress?

About a week before the wedding.

When do I need to return it?

Up to one week after the wedding.

I'm planning to lose weight before the wedding. What happens then?

Many brides lose weight between the first appointment and delivery. In any case of body changes, we alter and adjust the dress accordingly.

I found a dress but want to make changes to it?

With pleasure! The rental price includes minor alterations such as neckline changes, narrowing or shortening sleeves, belt changes, and length adjustments. For more significant changes, a separate quote will be provided after assessing the scope of the work.

I want a custom-tailored dress but I'm not sure what I want?

Our team includes skilled and experienced designers who will guide you through the process. It's flexible. you get to discover what you love and make decisions as you go.

Can I get ready at the salon on the wedding day?

Yes! The salon has a private bridal suite, and our team will accompany you calmly and professionally until you leave for the event.
