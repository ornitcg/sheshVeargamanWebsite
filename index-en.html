About Us

Shesh Ve'argaman bridal salon was established over 30 years ago
by Yael Cohen Gindi, an expert designer and seamstress.
The salon features a large workshop, fitting rooms,
a fabric, scarves and jewelry shop, and even bra fitting for the perfect look
All dresses are tailored by a skilled team of designers and seamstresses ,
custom-made for each bride or from our ready-made collection
We take pride in high-quality craftsmanship
using premium fabrics and maximum comfort
You will be welcomed by a warm and dedicated team
who will do everything to help you find your dream dress

Directions

Our Address

7 HaTirosh Street
Hashmonaim

Navigate with Waze

By Public Transit

From Jerusalem: 'Kavim' lines 310, 320, 330, 340
From Bnei Brak: 'Kavim' lines 210, 220, 240

Get off at the Hashmonaim Junction stop
and walk approx. 5 minutes
After entering Hashmonaim, turn right at the first roundabout
We are in the third house on the left
The salon is upstairs — use the staircase on the right side of the building

Accessible Entrance & Parking

For visitors with mobility impairments — there is an accessible entrance at the rear of the building.
Navigate to HaTamar Street and park in the plaza at the end of the street.
A marked disabled parking space is available.
Follow the accessible path to the left entrance of the building.
